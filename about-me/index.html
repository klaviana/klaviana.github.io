Hi, I'm Kyle.

Currently I am a Master's student at Stanford University studying Music, Science, and Technology at the Center for Computer Research in Music and Acoustics (CCRMA). Previously, I completed a B.S. at Stanford in Symbolic Systems with a concentration in interactive design, completing coursework in computer science, cognitive science, and applied statistics.


I am passionate about the intersection between design, technology, and music, and my current research explores their integration. Recent projects include an interactive web program for music education and a music controller that features unique degrees of musical expression. Projects I intend to explore this coming year include designing algorithms for artifically intelligent music composition as well as creating VR spaces for musical expression.


The past three summers I have worked as a counselor at Stanford Sierra Camp, where I have come to appreciate the importance of human-centered design (and made some of my best friends along the way). I am always looking to meet new people and hear about what they are passionate about. If you think we have something in common or just want to chat, please don't hesitate to send me a message at kyle@laviana.me!
