Hi, I'm Kyle.

This website contains a portfolio of research and development I did while pursuing an M.A. in Music Science and Technology from Stanford CCRMA, the Center for Computer Research in Music and Acoustics. At CCRMA I had the opportunity to study audio signal processing, embedded systems, ambisonics, and design for audio-visual software applications. Previously, I completed a B.S. at Stanford in Symbolic Systems, completing interdisciplinary coursework in cognitive and computer science.


My research lies at the intersection of music, technology, and user-centered design. I enjoy the creation of products from the initial ideation stage, through prototyping and user research, to developing and implementing algorithms. I adhere strongly to the philosophy of lateral thinking, wherein it is considered better practice to iterate through ideas early in the process rather than go down the rabbit hole only to realize the design itself is flawed.


Currently I am focused on novel means of music creation — developing software plug-ins, tinkering with analog gear, and building my portfolio. If you have a professional or personal inquiry, you can send me a message at kyle@laviana.me. Or .. check out luhkee.biz.
